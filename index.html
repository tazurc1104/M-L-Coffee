About us

Who are we?

M&L Coffee was founded on February 14, 2020 in Paranaque, Philippines by Laarni B. Marquez, a coffee enthusiast and entrepreneur. Our business specializes in online sales of high-quality drip bag coffee and ground coffee, sourced from the finest coffee beans and roasted to perfection. We take pride in providing our customers with a rich and flavorful coffee experience, while also promoting sustainability and ethical practices in the coffee industry. At M&L Coffee, we are committed to delivering a seamless online shopping experience and exceptional customer service.
